• Depozitha Enables Payment System's through Micro ATM Technology.

    Depozitha is a system that enables to carry out transactions through an App. It helps all individuals carry out financial transactions on a Micro-ATM and helps in empowering all sections of the society by making financial services available through the app.

    Visit a Micro ATM Agent or Informal shop (Spaza):

    Locate a micro ATM agent or a filling station that offers micro ATM services. These agents are often found in rural or remote areas.

    Provide Your Depozitha Details:

    Provide your Depozitha details to the micro ATM agent.This may include your Depozitha unique number or identifier.

    Provide the Amount to Deposit:

    Authenticate your transaction using your biometric information (fingerprint) and/or your depozitha unique number. The agent will guide you through the authentication process.

    Confirmation:

    Once the transaction is complete, the micro ATM will generate a online receipt as proof of your deposit. Verify the details on the receipt for accuracy.

    It's important to note that the specific steps and requirements may vary depending on the micro ATM provider see our merchats for more clarity. Additionally, micro ATMs are more commonly used in regions with limited access to traditional banking services.

    Uses of Depozitha

    The basic transactions that can be done through Depozitha are:

    • • Top Up Wallet
    • • Deposit into your bank account and other merchants.
    • • Cash Send - next sprint under discussions for API integration.
    • • Intrawallet or Interwallet fund transfer
    • • Balance enquiry
    • • Obtain a mini statement
    • • A payment system where payments can be made either directly to a bank account or to a proxy, such as a mobile phone number, that is associated with an account or wallet at any bank. This system provides flexibility in payment methods
    • • Payment to any merchant Benefits of Depozitha The Payment System enables the underbanked sections of the society to enable financial and basic services at ease.

    The benefits associated with Depozitha are:

    • 1. The account holder can do both, financial and non-financial transactions. Through a wallet in the depozitha using a voucher top up from our distribution partners E.g. (Kazang or flash Dealers) or direct top up (paperless system)
    • 2. It is a completely safe and secure process.
    • 3. It increases the reach to rural areas as banking executives can now reach distant rural places with micro POS machines.
    • 4. The process is easy-to-use for individuals who are not tech-savvy.
    • 5. It brings different sections of the society under a financial umbrella.
    • 6. In future Depozitha will helps in facilitating disbursements of different government schemes such as Social Security pension, Handicapped Old Age Pension etc using Depozitha. Requirements for Depozitha transactions Although Depozitha Payment System does not require any paper, it does require the individual to FICA the App account and mobile cell phone device. The App is meant to be zero rated as it will not use data to complete such transactions.

    How to use Depozitha?

    • Step 1: Visit a banking correspondent (informal Shop, Retailers or Fuelling station) in your nearest area. It is not necessary that he/she is a correspondent from your bank.
    • Step 2: Buy a discounted voucher.
    • Step 3: Enter your 12-digit Unique Identity Number on your app./ direct top up.
    • Step 4: Select the type of transaction- Cash deposit, Cash Send, Intrawallet or Interwallet fund transfer, Balance enquiry, obtain a mini statement or make a payment.
    • Step 5: Choose the bank name.
    • Step 6: Enter the transaction amount.
    • Step 7: As the transaction is completed in seconds, collect the electronic receipt as trail.

    Charges for Depozitha transactions The transactions with Depozitha are comparatively costly.

    In fact, costs can go up-to R15 per transaction. Transfer Limit for Depozitha. There is no set limit for transactions made through the Depozitha.However, certain banks will cap the transaction limit to avoid misuse of this system. Various banks may set a daily limit of R100,000 on total transactions.

    The Reason Behind Depozitha.

    Load shedding has become part of our daily lives affecting so many transactions especially ATM based and creating long unbearable queues. Part of this mandate is that we have decided to bring all the unbanked sections of the society into the banking framework. However, this is not geographically possible for people who live in distant and remote places. In order to provide them access to banking facilities, Depozitha is where people can easily send/receive money and avail banking and non-banking facilities with the help of micro-ATMS and distribution partners. This system is very inclusive and may help distribute social grants in future.
